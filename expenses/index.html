Task 1

The most important usability dimension for this app is to see who owes whom what, and easily tracking the expenses for each of them. There are a few usability problems with this spreadsheet. I was very confused on what the descriptions were and where the numbers came from. This makes the learnability low. Everything is clustered together and doesn't discernably separate Neo's and Trinity's expenses. It is difficult to read and understand. This makes the spreasheet inefficient. The information structure is unorganized and prone to mistakes, which affects the safety. Additionally, two of the columns display the same information (Neo owes and Variables).
